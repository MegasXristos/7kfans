Seven Kingdoms: Ambition

An updated and enhanced version of Seven Kingdoms: Ancient Adversaries — with numerous fixes, quality-of-life improvements and new features.

Note: Ambition is a separate project from the main 7KAA game. It builds on the same foundation but adds its own enhancements. It is maintained independently on SourceForge.
⚔️

What is Ambition?

Seven Kingdoms: Ambition is a real-time game of epic strategy and empire building featuring economy management, warfare, and intrigue. It is an enhanced fork of 7KAA with numerous fixes and improvements.

Begin with a single modest village and an entire world to conquer — by force of arms, guile, persuasion, or whatever means you choose. Wage war, forge alliances, send spies, manage trade, research technology, or seek the ultimate power: the aid of the gods.

✨

Enhancements Over 7KAA

  • Scrolling with mouse wheel, touchpad, and keyboard keys in Kingdom, Village, Economy and Trade panels
  • Unlimited production queues for War Factories and Harbours
  • Production queue size shown over the production button
  • War machine type and level displayed inside forts for easy identification
  • Command-range indicators for units outside their general's range
  • Unit leadership effect status shown in the info pane
  • Many UI fixes and refinements to info panels and labels
  • Stays compatible with 7KAA releases (latest merges in 2.15.7 changes)
