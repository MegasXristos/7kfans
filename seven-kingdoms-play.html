Play With Your Friends

The game supports up to 7 players over the internet. Your most memorable moments will come from playing against real opponents.

1 Download and install the latest version of the game. Old 2.0 versions from the 90s had desync issues and are no longer used. All players must use the same version.
2 Register a forum account on 7kfans.com. This lets you list your game so others can find and join it.
3 Launch the game, create a new match and select 7kfans.com as your service. This enables automatic NAT traversal — you can play even behind a firewall without port forwarding.
4 Find opponents in the forums or community channels. There is an active community of players eager to play.

Other Ways to Connect

Beyond 7kfans.com, the game also supports LAN and direct connect. These work well for private matches or when using VPN software.

Note: VPN software does not fix issues when playing over the open internet — use 7kfans.com for that.

🔧

Troubleshooting

Most connection issues come from overly strict firewall settings. Allow 7kaa.exe through your firewall and make sure traffic on the following ports is not blocked:

UDP Port 1
19255
UDP Port 2
19383
Port Forwarding
Not required
Max Latency
500 ms

The ports may shift if already in use. Note that guides written before version 2.15 use different port numbers and no longer apply.

As a peer-to-peer game, the max latency between players for smooth gameplay is 500 ms. Connection speed does not matter on modern broadband.
